Bring Me — a free 3D multiplayer party game

Everyone hides one object somewhere in a shared 3D world. Each round a jumbotron announces one item, and it's a mad scramble to deliver it to the referee first — walk it over or hurl it across the yard. Deliver someone else's item to score; keep your own hidden to score double.

How to play

  • Move with WASD, jump with Space, drag to orbit the camera.
  • Grab (E), drop (G), and throw (hold F) objects.
  • Stun rivals (Q) to make them fumble what they're carrying.
  • Play with friends in seconds — no download, no account, just share a room link.
